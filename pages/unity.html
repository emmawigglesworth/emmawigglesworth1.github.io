Game Development in Unity



After a great experience working with Pygame and developing a strong interest in the field of game development, I took the initiative to introduce myself to more advanced software in order to expand my skills and began familiarizing myself with Unity.

I have spent much of my time watching tutorials and reading about the software and its functionality. So far I have created a working game from scratch as well as explored more complex projects such as multiplayer FPS games. I would read through advanced scripts and make edits to practice and prove my understanding to myself.
